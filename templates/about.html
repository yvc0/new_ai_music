Our
Story

We’re a small team of music lovers and innovators making great-sounding masters fast, simple, and affordable for everyone.

Our Mission

Our mission is to assist budding independent artists and small creative in making their tracks sound polished and industry ready—without big budgets or complex tools. Every creative in this space deserves a clean, loud, and balanced master. MIXR.ai is the perfect free tool to help them achieve this goal.

Pranamya Shukla

Founder

“I go by the stage name Artemis and I am one of the most experimental and fearless producers in the underground DHH scene. I started out crafting sounds in small, makeshift studios with nothing but headphones and an obsession for pushing boundaries. Mixing and mastering always felt like a wall for independent artists, so I built MIXR.ai, a tool that makes the ‘last mile’ of a track effortless. It’s about giving every artist that final 10% of polish without the stress, so the focus stays on creativity, not limitations.”
