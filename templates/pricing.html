How It Works (Preview)

Signal Analysis & Chain

We analyze core audio features (balance, peaks, loudness) and apply gain staging, dynamics (look-ahead limiting), and sensible loudness targeting (typically around −14 to −16 LUFS for streaming-friendly playback).

Performance (Internal Tests)

In our internal testing, most tracks preview in seconds and maintain clear, punchy output with predictable loudness. We’ll keep tuning for edge-cases during beta.

Privacy & Data Handling (Beta)

  • Transport: Use HTTPS/TLS in deployment. We don’t claim “end-to-end encryption.”
  • Storage: Files are processed server-side for previews. Avoid uploading sensitive material.
  • Roadmap: Auto-deletion, clearer retention policy, and admin controls are planned.
  • Transparency: We won’t claim SOC 2 or similar certifications until they’re actually completed.
